This approach allows users to access and navigate the content comfortably on everything from large desktop monitors to small smartphone screens. Responsive design prioritizes user experience by optimizing layout, images, and functionality to fit each device's capabilities, enhancing accessibility and usability

Warranty coverage for an iPhone varies depending on the company and the specific terms and conditions of the warranty agreement.
